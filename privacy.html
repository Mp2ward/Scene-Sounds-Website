Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Practical Development Ltd

🔒 Scene Sounds is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your saved scenarios stay on your device.

1. Who We Are

Scene Sounds is operated by Practical Development Ltd, Company Registration No. 14228504, registered in England and Wales. Our contact email is info@scenesounds.app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we may collect responsibly and in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. What Data We Collect

Scene Sounds is a client-side web application. The vast majority of functionality runs entirely within your browser. We collect minimal data:

  • No account required — you do not need to register or log in to use Scene Sounds
  • No tracking cookies — we do not use advertising or tracking cookies
  • No personal data stored on our servers — your saved scenarios are stored in your browser's local storage only
  • Contact email — if you email us at info@scenesounds.app, we will retain your email address to respond to your enquiry
  • Feedback forms — if you submit feedback via our Google Form, Google's privacy policy applies to that data

3. Local Storage

Scene Sounds uses your browser's local storage to save your custom scenarios and preferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data
  • Is not linked to any personal identifier

4. Cookies

Scene Sounds does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. We may use essential browser storage (local storage, session storage) solely to make the app function correctly — for example, remembering your saved scenarios between sessions.

5. Third Party Services

Scene Sounds may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — we load fonts from Google's CDN. Google may collect limited technical data in accordance with their privacy policy
  • GitHub Pages — our app is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs
  • Google Forms — our optional feedback form is hosted by Google. If you submit feedback, Google's privacy policy applies

We do not sell or share any data with third parties for commercial purposes.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

As we hold minimal personal data (email enquiries only), most of these rights are straightforward to exercise. Contact us at info@scenesounds.app with any request.

7. Data Retention

We retain email enquiries for up to 12 months. We do not retain any other personal data. Local storage data on your device persists until you clear it.

8. Children's Privacy

Scene Sounds is intended for use by professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Continued use of Scene Sounds after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
